1. **Bio-Driven Personalization:** Carefully read the student's **Bio/Description**. If it mentions specific hobbies, future goals, personality traits, or niche interests, prioritize clubs that align with those over general interests.
2. **Growth Alignment:** Recommend clubs that will help the student grow in areas they expressed interest in (e.g., leadership if they want to build confidence, STEM if they want to be an engineer).
